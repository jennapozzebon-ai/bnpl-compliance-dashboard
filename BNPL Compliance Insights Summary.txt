# BNPL Compliance Insights Summary

## 📊 Overall Statistics
- **Total Transactions Analyzed:** 50,000
- **Overall Average Default Rate:** ~20.0%
- **Affirm Default Rate:** 24.50% (lowest among providers)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 1. Riskiest Purchase Categories
Travel and Electronics have the highest default rates at **18.15%** and **18.13%**, significantly above the 16.7% average. These discretionary categories warrant enhanced monitoring and potential additional verification steps.

### 2. Income-Based Risk Outlier
The **$30K-$50K income bracket** shows unexpectedly high risk at **27.47%** — nearly 8 percentage points above the overall average. This segment should be prioritized for underwriting reviews and possible adjustments to credit limits.

### 3. Provider Benchmarking
| Provider | Default Rate | Transaction Volume |
|----------|--------------|-------------------|
| Sezzle | 25.44% | 12,501 |
| Afterpay | 25.44% | 12,536 |
| Klarna | 24.62% | 12,545 |
| **Affirm** | **24.50%** | **12,418** |

Affirm has the **lowest default rate among BNPL providers**, outperforming competitors by nearly a full percentage point. This suggests effective underwriting standards.

### 4. Age Group Trends
| Age Group | Default Rate | Transaction Volume |
|-----------|--------------|-------------------|
| 18-25 | 19.50% | 8,421 |
| 26-35 | 20.26% | 10,802 |
| 36-45 | 21.10% | 10,717 |
| 46-55 | 20.19% | 10,527 |
| 56+ | 18.95% | 9,533 |

Customers aged **36-45** represent the largest transaction volume with above-average default rates — a key demographic for compliance monitoring.

### 5. High-Risk Transaction Watchlist
The dashboard includes a watchlist of transactions flagged for:
- Defaulted or late payment status
- High-risk categories (Travel, Electronics)
- High-risk income bracket ($30K-$50K)
- High transaction amounts

These flags support compliance teams in prioritizing reviews and escalations.

---

## 🎯 Actionable Recommendations

1. **Category-Level Monitoring:** Implement additional verification for Travel and Electronics purchases above $500
2. **Income-Based Underwriting:** Review credit criteria for the $30K-$50K income bracket, which shows outlier risk
3. **Provider Benchmarking:** Conduct monthly competitor analysis to monitor relative risk positioning
4. **Age Cohort Focus:** Prioritize the 36-45 age group for compliance outreach and monitoring
5. **Automated Alerts:** Design rules to flag transactions meeting multiple risk criteria for immediate review
